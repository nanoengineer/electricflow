welcome to "electric.flow",
an audiovisual interactive experience exploring the relationship between motion, agency, and aesthetics.

explore and play around with one hand in the view of the camera and discover how you can control the flow of the particles! :)
keyboard commands
[ space ] : fullscreen
[ c ] : camera on/off
[ O ] : toggle all overlays
[ 1 ] : toggle hand mirror
[ 2 ] : toggle camera display
[ 3 ] : toggle info panel
